why this exists

A Curiosity Cabinet
for Two

Distance is weird. You can FaceTime someone across the world but you can't steal a fry off their plate. These games won't fix that — but they'll give you something better than "wyd" and "nm u."

Think of this as a shared field journal. A place to collect small adventures, stupid dares, and the kind of inside jokes that only make sense to two people who'd rather be in the same room.

Every game here is designed to be played asynchronously, with nothing but a phone and a willingness to be a little ridiculous.
